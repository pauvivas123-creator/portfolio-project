## Summary
This interview-style case study shows the results of a cold email engagement after 60 days, highlighting booked meetings, meeting quality, ICP fit, and the operational complexity of running outbound correctly.

- Cold email can generate consistent meetings when executed properly
- Meeting quality matters more than raw volume
- Testing new verticals can unlock new growth opportunities
- Infrastructure and deliverability make cold email operationally difficult
